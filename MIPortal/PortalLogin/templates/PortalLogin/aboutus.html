About Us

MCRED is a platform powered by AI, Machine Learning and Data Analytics. It is designed to enable SMEs to take the advantage of power of discounting, for investors to
diversify their portfolio by investing in alternate asset class with lower risk and higher than expected returns. MCRED used in-house curated algorithm to identify and
analyse the requirements of business and investors. MCRED recommends provides the optimum solution for both business and the investor.

MCRED is promoted in by Man Investment Services Pvt. Ltd. Man Investments Group is an Investment and Financial Services Provider to Corporates, High Networth
Individuals, Fund Houses and Institution since 1997. MISPL has been active in the areas of bills discounting, invoice financing , Corporate finance and other financial
services since 23 years.

The group is promoted by Mr. Kamal Mehta, a Chartered Accountant and Mrs. Manisha Mehta, an MMS-Finance. Both have vast experience in the areas of Investments
and Corporate Financing. Their ability to offer suitable solutions under varied situations have helped them gain the trust and confidence of their clients. The group caters
to its geographically diverse clients from its premises at Mumbai.
